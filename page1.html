How can I get started?

    You are going to need the following:
  • a Rulebook for the game you want to play
  • some multi sided dice (in most Systems)
    • 4 sided
    • 6 sided
    • 8 sided
    • 10 sided
    • 12 sided
    • 20 sided
  • some friends who also want to play with you
  • willingness to act out your character
  • and general fun
    • if you don't have the opportunity to get a Rulebook there are websites for some games.
